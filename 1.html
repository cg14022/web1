How to 80/20 your life 이 뭐길래

You can get what you want with less. It's all about identifying where to make a split.

And of course, the 80/20 principle can still be applied to productivity at work. What tasks do you spend 80% of the time doing that bring in 20% of the returns?



Obviously, the 80/20 rule is not necessarily a rigid dictum to live by. But think of it as a tool, a lens to veiw aspects of your life through. Sit down and think about it, maybe even write it out. You'll likely be surprised with the realizations you come to.
